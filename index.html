Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"
Innovation
Driving progress through creativity and cutting-edge technology

Enhancing connectivity and development with modernized systems.
Empowerment

The Vision of Future
Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.
Digital Transformation
Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.
Green Energy
Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.
Modern Infrastructure
Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The world we live in has been designed to concentrate wealth and resources into the hands of a few while leaving billions behind. This isn’t a flaw—it’s a feature of the system we built. If we want a fairer world, we have to redesign that system entirely, creating institutions and businesses that prioritize people and planet over profit.

Social business is the bridge between the heart and the mind—between compassion and creativity. It takes the selfless desire to help others and combines it with the entrepreneurial spirit to create sustainable solutions to the world’s problems. It’s not about giving back; it’s about changing the system so that it never takes away in the first place.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
